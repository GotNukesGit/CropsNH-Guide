Once parents reach ≥80% growth they stay there while breeding — you're not harvesting. Growth only matters for the initial ramp-up. Probability depends only on parent species, pool memberships, and soil.
Environment
Environment affects nutrient score → crop growth speed and health. It does not affect mutation probability. At 31/31/31 with mature parents, these only matter for keeping crops healthy enough to keep breeding.
